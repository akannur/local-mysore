About us


We are a bunch of people from Mysore and one thing which was common among us was the passion towards Mysore, because of which we ended up creating this portal which will be helpful for the people of Mysore. The main idea is to make our life simple, instead of having all the bookmarks in various places with various browser accounts, we thought of having it in one single place which is Local Mysore. In this process, we thought of sharing our work with the public and thats how you are seeing this.

If you have any suggestions or idea to improve this portal, we are always open to hear from you and improve this portal. Please use the contact form to post your feedbacks or suggestions.

Do share and make others life simple.
